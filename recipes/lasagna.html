Lasagna

The lasagna is an italian dish comprised of multiple layers of pasta, with tomato sauce, meat, and cheese in between said layers.

It is a good dish to have on a cold day or thanksgiving.

Ingridients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Meat sauce (ground meat, tomato sauce, onions, and seasonings)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauces
    • Cook a savory meat sauce by browning ground meat, adding tomato sauce, garlic, and seasonings
    • Make a creamy Bechamel sauce or mix ricotta cheese with an egg, Parmesan, and herbs
  2. Preheat and Prepare Baking Dish
    • Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Farenheit (190 degrees Celcius)
    • Lightly grease a baking dish to prevent sticking.
  3. Layer the Lasagna
    • Start a think layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the dish.
    • Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
    • SPread a layer of Bechamel or ricotta mixture, followed by meat sauce.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top and repeat until the dish is full, and end with cheese on top.
  4. Bake the Lasagna
    • Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes to brown the cheese.
  5. Rest and Serve
    • Let the Lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
    • Enyour your delicious homemade lasagna!
