Fried Rice

The fried rice is theorized to come from a plethora of different countries

But it's cuisine is still unmatched, as it's only rice, but fried!... in a way.

Ingridients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (pref. a day old)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions (optional) for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble them. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil to the pan. Saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Mix thoroughly to coat the rice evenly.
  6. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and stir everything together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions, if desired. Serve hot!
